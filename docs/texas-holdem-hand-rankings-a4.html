Tie-breaking & kickers ការប្រៀបធៀបពេលដូចគ្នា និង kicker

Suits (♠ ♥ ♦ ♣) ឈាង ឬ suit

  • Standard Texas Hold'em: All suits are equal. Suit order never decides the winner at showdown.
  • If two players have the same best five ranks (same category and same kickers), the pot is split — e.g. both hold Ace-high flush with identical ranks: chop, even if one is spades and one is hearts.
KM: ក្នុងតេចាស់ ហូលដឹមស្តង់ដារ ឈាងបួនមិនមានឋានៈខ្ពស់ទាបទេ។ មិនអាចនិយាយថា ♠ ឈ្នះ ♥។ បើកាតប្រាំដូចគ្នាទាំងស្រុង បំណែក (pot) ចែកពាក់កណ្តាល (chop)។

Same hand type — comparison order ដូចគ្នាតាមប្រភេទ

  • Royal / straight flush: Higher top card of the straight wins (RF ties only chop).
  • Four of a kind: Higher quad, then kicker.
  • Full house: Higher three-of-a-kind, then higher pair.
  • Flush / straight / high card: Compare highest card, then next down, until one card differs.
  • Three of a kind: Higher trips, then higher kicker, then next kicker.
  • Two pair: Higher top pair → higher second pair → kicker.
  • One pair: Higher pair, then three kickers in order.
KM: ប្រៀបធៀបតាម “កាតសំខាន់” មុន រួចបន្តតាមកាតជំនួយ (kicker) ពីខ្ពស់ទៅទាប រហូតរកឃើញភាពខុសគ្នា។

Case Winner logic ខ្មែរ
Both flush, same high cards down to the 5th Split pot (suits irrelevant). ចែកបំណែក — ឈាងមិនចាំបាច់
Both flush, A J 9 6 3 vs A J 9 6 2 Second wins (higher 5th card: 3 > 2). ឈ្នះដៃដែលមានកាតទី 5 ខ្ពស់ជាង (3 > 2)
Both one pair K K, kickers 10 7 4 vs K K 10 8 4 Second wins (first kicker after pair: 8 > 7). ប្រៀបធៀប kicker ដំបូងបន្ទាប់ពីគូ (8 > 7)
Both two pair A A 9 9 K vs A A 9 9 Q Split (both pairs identical); compare kicker: K > Q → first wins. គូពីរដូចគ្នា ប្រៀប kicker — K ឈ្នះ Q
Same full house J J J 4 4 vs Q Q Q 4 4 Second wins (higher three-of-a-kind). ប្រៀបបីដូចគ្នាមុន — Q ឈ្នះ J
Texas Hold'em: best five of seven (2 hole + 5 community). For the button or seat draw, the house may use one suit order — that is not used for hand strength.
